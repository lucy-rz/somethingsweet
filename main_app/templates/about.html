About sweetness

Something sweet or something salty? That's not the question, but the answer is indeed something sweet!

The idea of "something sweet" is the result of those memories, when as a 90's kid when I use to go to "la tiendita", to buy my favorite candies.

Early this year while browsing for documentaries, I found that this activity used to be also japanese kids from the same generation and before; but instead of 'dulces', they would go and get 'dagashi'.
